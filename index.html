Privacy Policy

At CoreBot, we value and prioritize the privacy of our users. We understand how important it is to keep your personal information safe and secure. This policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard it when you use our Discord bots.

Information We Collect

We want to be clear that we do not collect any personal information from our users. However, message contents and audit logging information are stored on our servers for the sole purpose of providing the intended functionality of the bots. This means that when you use our Discord bots, we collect and store data related to the specific actions you take with the bots, such as commands you send and events that are logged.

How We Use Your Information

At CoreBot, we are committed to using the data we collect solely for the intended functionality of our bots. We do not use your information for any other purpose, and we do not share, sell, or distribute your information to third parties. The information we collect is used to provide the intended functionality of our bots, such as responding to commands and logging events.

Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously and use various measures to ensure it is protected from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. All data is stored on our servers with data-at-rest encryption, and only authorized personnel have access to it. We have also implemented policies and procedures to ensure that any data breaches are detected and addressed in a timely and appropriate manner.

Data Retention

We retain message contents and audit logging information on our servers for as long as necessary to provide the intended functionality of the bots. We may also retain such information for longer periods if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

GDPR Compliance

At CoreBot, we are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant data protection laws. This includes allowing our users to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information. If you would like to make such a request, please contact us via the email address provided on our website. We will respond to your request as soon as possible..

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. If we make any material changes to our policy, we will notify users by posting a notice in the #bot-updates channel on our Discord server. By continuing to use our bots after any changes to the policy, you agree to the revised terms.

Removal of User Data

At the request of the user, CoreBot can remove their data from our servers. You can request data removal in the CoreBot Discord Server
